Jaya Shiva Shankarah II

05 May 2017, 11:10
Guitar

This is a tune by John Fahey that was originally recorded as a duet with Woody Mann. The original recording can be found on two Fahey albums: Old Fashioned Love (Takoma C1043, 1975) and The Return Of The Repressed: Anthology (Rhino R2 71737, 1994). Here I have arranged the tune for solo guitar and have added a few more sections. Fahey’s liner notes on this piece, from Return of the Repressed, read: “A duet with Woody Mann.
