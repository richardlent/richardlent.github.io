St. Louis Blues

Fingerstyle guitar solo

09 Jun 2017, 09:32
Guitar

The song, not the hockey team (which, however, was named after the song). Written in 1914 by W. C. Handy, Saint Louis Blues has a long history and has been performed by Cab Calloway, Louis Armstrong, Ella Fitzgerald, Bessie Smith, and many others. The version I play here was arranged and recorded by John Fahey and can be found on several of his albums (Blind Joe Death, Vol. 1, Takoma C-1002; The Legend of Blind Joe Death, Fantasy TAK 8901, 1996; The Best of John Fahey, 1959-1977, Takoma 7058, 1977.) This is a live recording of me playing at Club Passim in Cambridge, Massachusetts, June 10, 2002. Recorded by Steve Friedman of Melville Park Studio.
